# MAS International Care, Business Overview

## Who is MAS International Care?

MAS International Care is a regional healthcare business development, market-entry, regulatory, and operational services company.

The company helps medical, pharmaceutical, healthcare, and related businesses enter new markets, establish local partnerships, register their products and companies, reach customers, participate in tenders, attract investors, and operate healthcare projects.

MAS acts as a bridge between international companies and the markets of Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Egypt, Syria, and the wider Middle East.

In simple terms, many international companies have strong medical products, technologies, or services but lack the local presence, regulatory knowledge, business relationships, and operational infrastructure required to work in the region. MAS provides that local support.

## Geographic presence

MAS operates through branches and business networks in:

* Saudi Arabia
* United Arab Emirates
* Egypt
* Syria

The company can support clients in one country or coordinate their expansion across several regional markets.

## Who does MAS work with?

MAS primarily serves:

* Medical-device manufacturers
* Pharmaceutical companies

* International companies entering Middle Eastern markets
* Hospitals and medical centers

* Healthcare investors
* Distributors and suppliers
* Government and private healthcare organizations
* Companies looking for local agents or commercial partners
* Entrepreneurs developing healthcare projects

# MAS Business Fields and Services

## 1. Local Agency and Company Representation

MAS represents international and regional companies that want to operate in markets where they do not yet have a local presence.

MAS can serve as the company’s local agent, representative, or market-entry partner. This allows the client to build a presence in the target country without having to manage every local process independently.

Services may include:

* Acting as a local representative for foreign companies
* Establishing and managing agency relationships
* Supporting the appointment or transfer of commercial agencies
* Representing the company before local partners and organizations
* Coordinating communication between the manufacturer and the local market
* Maintaining the company’s commercial presence in the country
* Identifying suitable distributors and business partners
* Supporting negotiations and partnership agreements
* Following up on local commercial opportunities
* Providing long-term representation after market entry

This service is suitable for a company that has a product or service but lacks a trusted local representative and business network.

## 2. Market Entry and Regional Expansion

MAS helps companies understand, enter, and expand within Saudi Arabia, the Gulf region, Egypt, Syria, and other Middle Eastern markets.

Before entering a new country, a company must understand its regulations, competition, pricing, customers, procurement system, and distribution structure. MAS helps the client prepare for these requirements.

Services may include:

* Market-entry strategy
* Target-market assessment
* Identification of suitable countries and market segments
* Competitor and opportunity analysis
* Evaluation of market demand
* Identification of regulatory and commercial requirements
* Entry-route recommendations
* Pricing and positioning guidance
* Local partnership strategy
* Distributor and customer identification
* Regional expansion planning
* Support with launching products and services
* Long-term market-development planning

The objective is to reduce market-entry risks and give the client a practical route into the region.

## 3. Marketing and Business Development for Other Companies

MAS promotes and develops the products and services of other companies within its target markets.

This is primarily a business-to-business service focused on building commercial relationships, creating market opportunities, and connecting clients with relevant buyers and partners.

Services may include:

* Marketing medical and pharmaceutical products
* Introducing products to hospitals, medical centers, distributors, and healthcare providers
* Identifying potential buyers and commercial opportunities
* Building relationships with decision-makers
* Organizing product presentations and business meetings
* Supporting product launches

MAS combines local market knowledge with regional business relationships to help clients reach the right organizations and decision-makers.

## 4. Regulatory Registration and Compliance Support

Medical, pharmaceutical, food, cosmetic, and healthcare-related products usually require registration and regulatory approval before they can be marketed or sold.

MAS helps companies understand and complete the relevant registration procedures in each market.

Services may include:

* Registration of medical devices
* Registration of pharmaceutical products
* Registration of food products
* Registration of cosmetics and other regulated products
* Preparation and coordination of registration documents
* Communication with the relevant regulatory authorities
* Follow-up on applications and regulatory requests
* Support with product classification
* Assistance with licensing requirements
* Coordination of document translation, legalization, and authentication
* Renewal and maintenance of registrations
* Support with regulatory updates and compliance requirements
* Assistance with establishment and importer licensing when applicable

In Saudi Arabia, this may include support involving the Saudi Food and Drug Authority, such as medical-device and other product registration procedures. Equivalent authorities and procedures are handled according to the client’s target country.

Final approval always remains subject to the requirements and decisions of the relevant government authority.

## 5. Government and Private Procurement Registration

MAS helps companies become eligible to sell to government bodies, hospitals, healthcare groups, and private purchasing organizations.

Services may include:

* Registration on government procurement platforms

* Supplier and vendor registration

* Uploading company and product documentation
* Supporting prequalification processes
* Maintaining and updating supplier profiles

# How MAS Supports a Client

A typical client journey may look like this:

1. A medical manufacturer wants to enter Saudi Arabia or another regional market.
2. MAS studies the company, its products, and its target market.
3. MAS recommends the best market-entry and partnership structure.
4. The company or product is prepared for the relevant regulatory registration.
5. MAS identifies an appropriate agent, distributor, investor, or operating partner.
6. The company is registered on relevant procurement and vendor platforms.
7. MAS introduces the products to hospitals, healthcare organizations, and potential buyers.
8. MAS supports tender opportunities, negotiations, and product launches.
9. MAS continues providing local representation and business-development support.
10. The same model can then be expanded into additional regional markets.
