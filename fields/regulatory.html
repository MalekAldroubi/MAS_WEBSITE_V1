Product Registration &
Government Tenders.

We register your products with the SFDA and other health authorities, list your company on procurement platforms like Etimad, and prepare your tender submissions.

How it works

Get registered. Get purchasable.

“A product that isn’t registered can’t be sold. A supplier that isn’t listed can’t win tenders.”

MAS manages the full journey of classification, documents, submissions, and authority follow-up, then registers your company where government and hospital buyers actually purchase, so you can bid and sell.

Health-authority registration

SFDA in Saudi Arabia and the equivalent authorities in the UAE, Egypt, and Syria.

Documents handled for you

Translation, legalization, submissions, renewals, and follow-up with the authority.

Tender-ready company

Etimad and platform registration, supplier files, and complete bid preparation.
