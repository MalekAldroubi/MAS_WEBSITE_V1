Our role

We handle the local side, so you can focus on your product.

Many international companies have strong products, but no local presence, no registrations, and no relationships in the region. That is exactly what we provide.

MAS acts as your official agent: we register your products with authorities like the SFDA, get your company onto procurement platforms like Etimad, introduce you to distributors, hospitals, and investors, and manage healthcare operations on the ground, across four markets, with one point of contact.
